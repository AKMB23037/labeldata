Who We Are

We are an IIT‑driven data services team delivering end‑to‑end labeling and master data solutions for AI and enterprise pipelines. Our process combines skilled annotators, robust guidelines, and multi‑stage quality checks to produce clean, consistent, and production‑ready datasets.

  • Domain coverage: computer vision, NLP, and speech.
  • Scalable team structure for enterprise timelines.
  • Transparent QA metrics and reviewer feedback loops.
